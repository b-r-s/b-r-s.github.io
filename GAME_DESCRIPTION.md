Checkers is a classic two-player strategy game played on an 8x8 board. Move your pieces diagonally and jump over your opponent's pieces to capture them. Reach the opposite end of the board to crown your pieces as Kings with enhanced movement abilities. The goal is to capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them from making any legal moves.

**ðŸ“Š Game Rules:**
- Regular pieces move diagonally forward one square
- Kings can move diagonally in any direction
- Capture opponent pieces by jumping over them
- Multiple jumps in one turn are allowed
- If a capture is available, you must take it
- First player to eliminate all opponent pieces wins
