# Privacy Policy for Checkers4Pi

**Effective Date:** January 20, 2026  
**Status:** Mainnet-Compliant (Data Minimization Policy)

At **Checkers4Pi**, we believe that your strategy should be the only thing we track. Our application is designed with a "Privacy-First" architecture to ensure Pioneers can enjoy the game without compromising their personal data.

---

## 1. Data Collection & Minimization
Unlike traditional mobile games, Checkers4Pi does **not** collect, store, or transmit the following:
* **No** Email addresses or Phone numbers.
* **No** Real names or physical addresses.
* **No** Device identifiers (IMEI/Serial Numbers).
* **No** Contacts or Gallery access.

## 2. Pi Network Authentication & Safety
We exclusively use the **Pi Network SDK** for authentication. 
* We only receive your unique **Pi Username** and **UID** provided by the Pi Browser. 
* This data is used solely to save your game progress and AI difficulty preferences.
* **Passphrase Security:** We never see, ask for, or store your Pi Wallet Passphrase. Checkers4Pi will **never** prompt you to enter your passphrase within the app interface.

## 3. Local Storage & Cookies
To optimize performance and save battery life:
* Game state (e.g., current piece positions) is stored **locally** on your device's cache.
* We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We have a strict **Zero-Sharing Policy**:
* Your data is never sold to ad networks or data brokers.
* There is no integration with Google Analytics or Facebook SDKs.

## 5. Security
All communications between the app and the Pi SDK are encrypted via the Pi Browser's secure layer. As a non-custodial application, we do not have access to your Pi assets.

## 6. Contact & Data Deletion
If you wish to reset your game data or have questions regarding our privacy practices, please contact us via our GitHub repository or the Pi Brainstorm project page.

*Checkers4Pi is an independent developer project and is not officially affiliated with SocialChain Inc. or the Pi Core Team.*
